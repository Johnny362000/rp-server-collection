Welcome to FiveReborn Freeroam.

Here a few tips and rules.

  • Every 2 minutes you get money which you can spend on items which you can find in /help
  • You can purschase businesses that have a yellow marker. Type /re in them to start.
  • You can rob places that have a black marker. Type /rob in them to rob them. Be careful the cops will come! You can run away whenever you want.
  • For personal vehicles type /pv to start.
  • Your cash can be viewed in the top-right corner of your screen.
  • Be sure to respect everyone, or an admin might come get ya.
  • You can join/create a crew for your friends if you want. Type /crew to start.

Now to get rid of this screen and to play!
Type "/register [password]" without quotes to register an account if you don't have one yet.
Type "/login [password]" without quotes to register an account if you already have one.
